Image Colorizer

The image and video colorizer allows you to use neural network technology to see your old black and white images or videos in color! Click upload below and select a jpg, png, or mp4 file of less that 5 mb.

This website colorizes black and white images using a neural network backend. Currently in development. Created for CSCI121 at Harvey Mudd by Miles Bernhard, Elakshi Shah, Kripesh Ranabhat, and Maria Croom.
